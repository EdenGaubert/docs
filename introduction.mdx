## Introduction

Cardster allows any product to seamlessly integrate card payments without the friction of traditionnal on-rampers (KYC, buy crypto, etc.) - Cardster generates a stripe checkout, buys the NFT through a hot wallet managed internally, and sends the NFT to the customer.

Developers only have to input the NFT ID, the receipient wallet, and voil√† - NFTs ready for mass adoption!
